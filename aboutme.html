About Me

Firstly, thank you for taking some time to visit my portfolio and checking out my projects! My name is Cindy Guzman and I'm a proud data-nerd. I graduated from George Mason University with a Bachelor's in Mathematics with a Statistics concentration. I have experience with programming languages such as R, Python, SQL and SAS and well-versed in Excel. I enjoy analytical problem-solving because it allows to me to use my knowledge and creativity simultaneously. I enjoy story-telling with data and creating visualizations that bring information to life for all to understand. I currently work as a Data Steward in the Data Management Operations department for Creative Artists Agency.


When I'm not problem-solving with data, I love to spend time with my family and friends. As a pastime, I weightlift, play soccer, dance and perform Salsa routines. I enjoy traveling to new places for the views, the experience and most importantly, the food!

Quote of the day: Data is like garbage. You'd better know what you are going to do with it before you collect it.
